Kinetic Energy is energy in movement associated with a particular reference frame

Translational Kinetic Energy
$\frac{1}{2}mv^2$

Springs:
$\frac{1}{2}kx^2$

[[Rotational Motion|Rotational]] Kinetic Energy:
$$
\begin{align}
KE&=\frac{1}{2}mv^2 \\
&=\frac{1}{2}mr^2\omega^2 \\
&=\boxed{\frac{1}{2}I\omega^2}
\end{align}
$$
