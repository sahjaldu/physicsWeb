<ins>**Linear**</ins>
$$
\begin{align*}
v&=v_{0}+at \\
x&=v_{0}t+\frac{1}{2}at^2 \\
v^2&=v_{0}^2 +2ax \\
\bar{v}&=\frac{v+v_{0}}{2}
\end{align*}
$$

<ins>**Angular**</ins>
$$
\begin{align*}
\omega&=\omega_{0}+\alpha t \\
\theta&=\omega_{0}t-\frac{1}{2}\alpha t^2 \\
\omega^2&=\omega_{0}^2+2\alpha\theta \\
\bar{\omega}&=\frac{\omega+\omega_{0}}{2}
\end{align*}
$$
